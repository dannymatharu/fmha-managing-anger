Safe support matters

Facebook groups are not the answer.

There’s no quality control. No moderation standard. No protection from hostile advice. One loud “Dave” can stop a good volunteer asking for help again.

Grassroots football has been trying to solve sensitive welfare issues in the least safe room on the internet.

The risk

You’re carrying legal risk you may not know about.

The Equality Act 2010 doesn’t wait for perfect confidence. Clubs need to understand reasonable adjustments, anticipatory duty and how to evidence what they’ve done.

“If you’re not documenting reasonable adjustments, you’re exposed.”
